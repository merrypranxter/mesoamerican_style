# Examples (text-only)

These are example *instructions* a generator can follow.

## Example 1 — Border band
- Build a step-fret border band 12 tiles wide.
- Alternate two variants every other tile.
- Base color: Limestone. Groove shadow: Obsidian. Accent: Jade.

## Example 2 — Glyph wall
- 5 rows of glyph blocks.
- Each block subdivided into 4–9 compartments.
- Randomly place: hook, S-curve, dot cluster, feather bundle.
- Apply relief shading and pigment-in-recesses.

## Example 3 — Serpent centerpiece
- Central S-curve serpent.
- Surround with two registers of small glyphs.
- Cinnabar highlights in the deepest channels.
